Welcome to the Crystal Collector Game!

Instructions

You will be given a random target number at the start of each round.

There are four crystals in the game box. By clicking on a crystal, you can add a specific amount of points to your current number.

You win the round by matching your current number to the target number, but if you exceed the target, you lose the game.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time a new round starts, the values of the crystals will change.
